WHAT IS 'hlutpay'?

Hlutpay = "လွှတ်ပေး" means 'to release' in Burmese.

MISSION

We are a group of enthusiastic volunteers trying to collect data and information in regards to the people who went missing or were detained under the terrorist military junta. Our mission is to help those who were the victim of this unlawfulness. It is our ambition to support and help them be released as soon as possible.

တရားမဝင်စစ်အာဏာရှင်များကြောင့်မတရား ထိန်းသိမ်းခံထားရ/ပျောက်ဆုံးနေသူများ အမြန်ဆုံးလွတ်မြောက်ရန်အတွက် သတင်းအချက်အလက်များစုဆောင်းပေးသော အဖွဲ့စည်းတစ်ခုဖြစ်ပါသည်။

USAGE

‘hlutpay’ will serve as a tool where lawyers, family members and supporters can obtain information on those who have been detained, i.e the location where they have been detained and where they were taken.This website is intended to be a portal for the public to view information on the arrests with the hope that we could help connect them with proper law authorities to aid their release.

ရှေ့နေများ၊ ထိန်းသိမ်းခံမိသားစုများနှင့် အကူညီပေးနိုင်သူများ အချင်းချင်းအဆက်အသွယ်ရရှိပြီး အချက်အလက်ရယူဝေမျှနိုင်ရန်။

DISCLAIMER

The data we collected is based on our individual collections and our facebook group, Missing people. We intend to create a reliable list of individuals who were detained or went missing so that it’s easier to seek aid for individual needs. As there are difficulties to contact the witness in some cases, the accuracy and totality of the information is not guaranteed.

အချက်အလက်အများစုမှာ Facebook မှသက်ဆိုင်ရာ မျှဝေသူများဆီမှ ရယူစုဆောင်းထားခြင်းဖြစ်သည်။
